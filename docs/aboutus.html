About

bookCritics is an online book review platform. This service connects the world's best critics with subscribers around a topic and allows them to post reviews on topics such as genre, books per month or overall popularity.

The reviews and recommendations will help you choose the best book for you. Our goal is to create a system that offers you the greatest amount of information. Reviews provide readers with valuable insight into how their favorite authors have used the products they review.

We believe books makes life better!

Everyone should read - reading can put our brains into a pleasurable trance-like state, similar to meditation, and it brings the same health benefits of deep relaxation and inner calm. Regular readers sleep better, have lower stress levels, higher self-esteem, and lower rates of depression than non-readers.

The greatest recommenders of books are people, not computer algorithms.

Online communities should be friendly, supportive environments with helpful admins and staff who really listen.
